Courses We Offer

We provide foundational and primary education under government affiliation. Our curriculum is designed to develop students holistically from Nursery to Class 8th.

Nursery

Early childhood education with focus on motor skills, phonetics, and basic interaction.

L.K.G.

Lower Kindergarten focuses on letters, numbers, colors, and rhymes.

U.K.G.

Upper Kindergarten builds reading readiness, vocabulary, and writing skills.

Class 1

Introduction to formal schooling: language, math, EVS, and values.

Class 2

Building on language skills and numeric concepts with practical examples.

Class 3

Introducing grammar, science, and basic computer education.

Class 4

Enhanced syllabus with introduction to social studies and analytical thinking.

Class 5

Focus on writing skills, arithmetic speed, and project-based learning.

Class 6

Subject-wise learning begins: English, Hindi, Maths, Science, and SST.

Class 7

Structured curriculum with interactive sessions and labs (if available).

Class 8

Prepares students for higher education with a strong academic foundation.
