About Us

H.D. Memorial Public School is a Hindi medium private institution committed to nurturing young minds from Nursery to Class 8. Established with a vision to provide accessible and meaningful education, the school was founded by Dr. Tribhuvan Nath Pandey, a respected Ayurvedic researcher and social reformer, whose lifelong dedication to knowledge and service has been recognized multiple times by the President of India and other esteemed national authorities.

Rooted in rural values yet aligned with the vision of modern education, the school is a humble yet determined response to the challenges of educational inequality. Dr. Pandey firmly believed that education is not a privilege but a right, and founded the school on the principles of the Right to Education Act, ensuring that even those with minimal resources could receive quality learning.

At H.D. Memorial Public School, we strive to create a safe, inclusive, and inspiring environment where every child is encouraged to grow intellectually, emotionally, and morally. With dedicated educators and a community-driven approach, we continue to carry forward our founder’s legacy—making education not just a service, but a mission of hope and transformation.
